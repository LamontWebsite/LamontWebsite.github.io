Spanish Class Exhibition

This is what we did in Spanish Exhibition.


We did a memorial for the people that deceased; Me and Choja were working on a memorial for Selena Quintanilla, the former latin american Music artist who lived in the state of Texas. Selena Quintanilla was a songwriter and singer from Lake Jackson, Texas. She became a superstar with her Latin music. She was named the Best Selling Latin Artist of the Decade by Billboard. She was brutally murdered on March 31, 1995 at the tragic age of 23. She was killed by the president of her fan club. Her life has since been adapted into a major motion picture entitled, Selena.
